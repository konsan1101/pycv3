Usage: python IdentifyFile.py <subscription_key> <identification_file_path> <profile_ids>...
	<subscription_key> is the subscription key for the service
	<identification_file_path> is the audio file path for identification
	<force_short_audio> True/False waives the recommended minimum audio limit needed for enrollment
	<profile_ids> the profile IDs for the profiles to identify the audio from.
